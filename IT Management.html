Understanding IT Support & Asset Management

What is IT Support & Asset Management
and why does it matter?

Before diving into features, let us understand the foundation — what a complete IT support and asset management system is, and why giving your team the right tools can make all the difference.

ZarklyX IT Support & Asset Management is a unified system built to empower your team with a seamless technical support experience and a high-performance asset tracker. When a technical glitch happens, your employees should not have to wait — the platform is designed to get your IT team working on a resolution in seconds.

The system is built around two core pillars: a quick-response ticketing engine that handles every tech issue from the moment it is reported to the moment it is resolved, and a comprehensive asset and service manager that gives you total oversight of every laptop, license, and tool in your organisation.

What makes ZarklyX different is that the entire workflow is designed to keep your team productive. Tickets are raised in seconds, assets are tracked with full ownership details, and renewal reminders ensure you are always ahead of expiry dates — whether you are managing assets for your own company or specifically for your clients.

Quick-Response IT Tickets

Tech issues solved before they
slow you down.

ZarklyX IT Support is not just a ticketing system — it is a connected workflow that takes an issue from report to resolution with complete visibility at every step for both the employee and the IT team.

Raise a Ticket

When a technical glitch happens, your employees should not have to wait. Our simplified ticketing system allows your team to report issues in seconds so the IT experts can get to work. Employees can easily provide their name, a description of the problem, set the priority level, and even add an attachment — like a screenshot — for better clarity. The entire submission process is designed to be fast and effortless, removing any barrier between the employee experiencing the issue and the IT team who can fix it.

Schedule Support

Not every issue needs to be fixed at this exact moment. ZarklyX allows employees to select a preferred date for the fix to ensure IT support does not interrupt important meetings or scheduled business activities. This scheduling capability gives both employees and the IT team the flexibility to plan resolutions efficiently, without forcing disruptions to the working day.

Track to Completion

Once a ticket is raised and the IT team begins working on it, both parties have full visibility of the progress at every stage. Watch the ticket move from the IT team's dashboard to Completed status once the problem is resolved. This end-to-end tracking ensures that no issue falls through the cracks, every resolution is recorded, and employees always know where their ticket stands in the support queue.
