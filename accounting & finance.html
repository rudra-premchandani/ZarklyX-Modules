Understanding Accounting & Finance

What is Accounting & Finance
and why does it matter?

Before diving into features, let's understand the foundation — what a complete accounting system is, and why managing every financial transaction with precision is non-negotiable for any growing business.

ZarklyX Accounting & Finance is a complete financial management system designed to handle every rupee that moves through your business — with precision, transparency, and zero paperwork headache. From the moment you raise a purchase order with a vendor to the final payment received from a client, every transaction is tracked, recorded, and reconciled in real time.

The system is built around two core financial flows: vendor and purchase management, which handles your supply chain, and sales and client invoicing, which manages your revenue cycle. Both flows are seamlessly connected through smart financial notes — Debit Notes and Credit Notes — that handle corrections, adjustments, and post-sale finalizations without disrupting your books.

What makes ZarklyX different is that the entire accounting workflow is designed for non-accountants. You do not need to understand double-entry bookkeeping to create a purchase order, convert it into a bill, and record a vendor payment. The system guides you step by step, keeping your accounts accurate even if you have never used accounting software before. From creating a Purchase Order to receiving items, converting to a Bill, and recording vendor payment — the entire procurement cycle is handled in one connected workflow with a full audit trail.

Core Capabilities

Everything you need for
complete financial control

ZarklyX Accounting & Finance is not a collection of disconnected tools — it is a single, integrated financial system where every module connects to the next, keeping your books accurate at every step.

Items & Services Catalogue

Easily add and categorize the products you buy or the services you use. A clean, well-organized catalogue ensures every purchase order and invoice references accurate, consistent line items across all your financial documents.

Purchase Orders

Creating a Purchase Order is simple for office essentials or bulk stock. Track exactly what you are buying before the bill arrives and maintain full vendor accountability with a clear paper trail. Once your items arrive, convert your Purchase Order into a Purchase Bill and record the Payment to keep your vendor accounts updated at all times.

Quotations & Client Invoicing

Send professional quotes to your clients. Once approved, turn them into Invoices with one click and track the Payment status in real-time through the entire collection cycle — from the initial quote to the final payment received.

Credit Notes for Clients

Use Credit Notes for sales returns or corrections. Whether there was a deficiency in service, an invoice error, or a Sales Return, you can easily adjust the balance for your client and maintain accurate receivables without disrupting the rest of your books.

Debit Notes

Debit Notes are available for both Vendors and Clients. Use these for invoice corrections, changes in Point of Sale data, or adjusting provisional assessments. A dedicated Post-Sale Dictionary provides a structured space to handle service deficiencies, price changes, or finalizations after the sale is complete.

Financial Control with ZarklyX

Smart Notes: Debit & Credit
made simple

Financial corrections can be tricky — ZarklyX makes them simple. Every adjustment, return, or correction is handled through a structured, auditable workflow that keeps your books clean.

Debit Notes — Vendors & Clients

Debit Notes in ZarklyX are available for both Vendors and Clients. Whether you need to correct an invoice, record a change in Point of Sale data, or adjust a provisional assessment, the system provides a structured workflow that documents every correction with full traceability. No adjustment is ever made silently — every debit note creates a clear financial record that is linked to the original transaction and visible in the audit trail.

Credit Notes — Sales Returns & Errors

When a client returns goods, disputes a charge, or receives less value than invoiced, a Credit Note is the correct financial response. ZarklyX makes it straightforward to generate a Credit Note tied to the original invoice, adjust the client's outstanding balance, and maintain accurate receivables — whether the reason is a service deficiency, a price correction, or a formal sales return.
