What is Token & Queue

Your complete command center
for every client request

Stop juggling emails and start managing workflows. Our Token system keeps every request organized, transparent, and moving toward completion.

Every Request Becomes a Token

Every task that enters your system is converted into a Token — a trackable, manageable unit of work with its own identity, history, and status. Rather than losing client requests in email threads, messaging apps, or scattered documents, ZarklyX captures every request the moment it arrives and gives it a clear place in your workflow. Nothing falls through the cracks, and nothing is forgotten.

A Single, Organized Queue

The Token system organizes all work into clear, actionable categories so you always know the precise status of every task at any given moment. New requests land in the Inbox and can be starred to highlight priority items, just like the starred function in email. Items waiting for client feedback sit in Pending, while tasks paused for any reason are moved to On Hold for later. Tokens currently being worked on appear in Processing, those awaiting final approval move to Review, and every completed task is preserved in the Completed archive — a satisfying record of every goal your team has achieved.

Client Change Requests — Tracked

When a client requests changes to a token, those changes are tracked within the system. Every change request is logged against the original token with a timestamp, a description of what was requested, and the current status of that change. This gives your team and your clients complete visibility into every revision, correction, or addition made throughout the lifecycle of a task.

How the System Works

From first request
to final completion

Every client task moves through a structured, transparent workflow — automatically tracked, always visible, and never lost.

1

Request Arrives as Token

Every new client request is captured and converted into a Token in the Inbox. It is assigned a unique ID, a client name, a request title, and a team member — ready to be actioned immediately.

2

Token Moves Through Stages

As work progresses, the token moves through stages — from Inbox to Starred, Pending, On Hold, Processing, or Review — reflecting exactly where it stands in the workflow at every moment.

3

Changes Are Tracked Live

When a client requests changes, those requests are logged directly against the token. The full history of every change, decision, and status update is preserved in the Token Timeline.

4

Completed & Archived

Once a token reaches Completed, it is archived with its full history, total time spent, and all change requests — providing a complete record for billing, performance review, and client transparency.

Workflow Management

Your Command Center
— the Inbox

Never miss a client request again. Every task is organized into clear, actionable categories so you always know the status of your work.

Inbox and Starred

New requests land in the Inbox the moment they are raised. From there, your team can immediately assign them, begin work, or mark them as Starred to flag them as priority items. The Starred function works similarly to starred email — it surfaces the most important tokens at a glance without removing them from the main workflow, ensuring high-priority work always gets the attention it deserves.

Pending and On Hold

Pending tokens are those waiting for client feedback — items where your team has reached a point that requires a client decision, approval, or additional information before work can continue. On Hold tokens are tasks that have been deliberately paused for a later time, whether due to resource availability, external dependencies, or a client's request to delay. Both states are clearly visible in the queue, so nothing is ever silently abandoned or forgotten.

Processing and Review

Processing shows exactly which tokens are currently being worked on by your team, along with who is assigned and how much time has been logged. Review is the stage where completed work awaits final client approval before being marked as done. Together, these two stages give managers and clients complete real-time visibility into active work and what is ready to be signed off.

Completed

The Completed section is a satisfying archive of every goal your team has checked off. Each completed token retains its full history, total time spent, all change requests, and every stage it passed through — making it a reliable reference for client billing, team performance reviews, and future project planning.

Token Timeline

The Token Timeline provides a complete view of the entire history of a task — from the moment it was raised to the second it was finished. Every stage transition, every change request, every assignment change, and every client interaction is logged chronologically. This gives both your team and your clients total transparency into how a task was handled, what decisions were made, and when each action took place.

Time Spent Tracking

Time tracking is automatic. ZarklyX calculates exactly how many hours or minutes were spent on each token across every stage of the workflow — without requiring team members to manually start or stop timers. This makes Time Spent Tracking perfect for accurate agency billing, giving you precise, defensible data for every invoice you send to a client. It also enables performance analysis, helping you understand which types of requests take the most time and where your team's capacity is being used.
